Who We Are

Bridging Yardi and Enterprise Banking

LogiPrime is a dedicated integration partner helping real estate firms automate treasury operations, reduce payment overheads, and completely eliminate vendor wire fraud.

Our Core Mission

For real estate finance and accounts payable (AP) teams, processing payments securely has become increasingly complex. Cyber threats like Business Email Compromise (BEC) target vendor onboarding workflows, while third-party Treasury Management Systems (TMS) charge steep subscription fees simply to transfer files to banks.

LogiPrime was founded to challenge this. We believe enterprise integrations should be direct, secure, and run natively inside your core systems. By building direct pipelines from Yardi Voyager to J.P. Morgan Chase's API and SFTP gateways, we remove intermediate servers, eliminating unnecessary transaction and software licensing fees.

Why LogiPrime Stands Out

  • No Subscription Fees: We operate on a flat, one-time implementation model, keeping your budget predictable.
  • Infrastructure-Light: Runs natively inside Yardi using standard Python tasks. Nothing new to procure or host.
  • Direct Banking Relations: Directly harness J.P. Morgan's native infrastructure (AVS, Chase Pay Source, and ISO 20022 XML pain message formats).
